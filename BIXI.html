SPORT

Sports play an important role in our lives by keeping us healthy, active, and disciplined. They help us build physical strength, improve confidence, and learn teamwork.
Playing sports like football, cricket, basketball, badminton, or volleyball teaches us cooperation and fair play. Sports also reduce stress and make our minds fresh by giving us a break from daily routines. Whether played for fun or professionally, sports bring people together, create excitement, and help develop a strong and positive attitude in life.

CRICKET

Cricket is a widely loved sport played between two teams, where one team bats and the other bowls and fields. The main goal of the batting team is to score runs, while the bowling team tries to get the batsmen out and limit the runs. The game requires skill, concentration, teamwork, and strategy. Cricket can be played in different formats like Test matches, One Day Internationals, and T20s, each offering its own excitement. Whether played professionally on large stadiums or casually on local grounds, cricket brings people together and creates a sense of joy, teamwork, and sportsmanship among players and fans.

VOLLEYBALL

Volleyball is an energetic and exciting sport played between two teams separated by a high net. The main objective is to hit the ball over the net in such a way that the opposing team cannot return it. Each team works together to pass, set, and spike the ball, which builds strong teamwork and coordination. Volleyball requires quick reflexes, good communication, and physical fitness. Whether played indoors, on the beach, or in a school ground, volleyball brings players together, encourages cooperation, and creates a fun and competitive environment for everyone involved.
